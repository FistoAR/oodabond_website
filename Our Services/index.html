Thread‑locking & Sealing

Thread-locking and sealing adhesives are used to secure threaded fasteners by filling microscopic gaps between the threads. Once applied, the adhesive hardens to lock the parts in place, preventing loosening caused by vibration. It also seals out moisture, oil, and other contaminants, protecting against rust and corrosion. This enhances the durability and reliability of mechanical assemblies.
