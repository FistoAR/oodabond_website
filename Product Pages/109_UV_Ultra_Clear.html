109 UV ULTRA CLEAR

OODA BOND 109UV is a high performance UV curing adhesive engineered to bond plastics, metal, and glass. It can be used in a variety of product assemblies and it promotes innovative design solutions. It is a fast curing adhesive and a leading performer when used for dome coating and Metals, glass bonding and potting applications.

OODABOND 109UV is a fast curing urethane acrylate that bonds well to a wide variety of different substrates. This product requires direct UV exposure during cure. The ability of this product also available to fluoresce under black light facilitates inspection of bonded assemblies for adhesive presence
