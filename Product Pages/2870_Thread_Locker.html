2870 THREAD LOCKER

OODA 2870 is a single component, high strength (permanent) anaerobic threadlocker. 2870 cures when confined in the absence of air between close-fitting metal surfaces.

OODA 2870 is formulated to lock metal fasteners and thus prevent vibration loosing and leakage through the threads. 2870 is particularly suitable for larger threaded fasteners and studs and all applications where maximum strength is needed. OODA 2870 prevents corrosion of assembled parts.
