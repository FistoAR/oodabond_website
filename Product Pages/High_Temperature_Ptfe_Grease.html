HIGH TEMPERATURE PTFE GREASE

OODA BOND PTFE – High Temperature Grease is a unique and innovative blend of activated PTFE (Poly Tetra Fluoro Ethylene) propriety additives and Lithium based water repellent grease. Being compatible with most existing lubricants, it can be safely used in almost any greasing application to improve lubrication and reduce temperature and wear and tear. OODA BOND Grease is a versatile product as it has a vast variety of applications ranging from Industries, Trucks/Buses, Construction equipment/machineries, Industrial equipments, Paint shop Conveyers, Furnace, Oven etc.

This multi-purpose OODA BOND High Temperature grease inculcates PTFE particles that are in the sizes of sub microns and hence tend to get finely and evenly dispersed.

BENEFITS &

FEATURES

Activated PTFE forms a protective film on metal surfaces, minimizing metal-to-metal contact.
Inhibits oxidation and protects metal parts from corrosion.
Prevents direct contact between parts, reducing mechanical degradation.
Reduces wear, allowing bearings to be reused and last significantly longer.
PTFE is chemically inert and non-staining — safe for varied environments.
Offers long-term lubrication even during intervals without reapplication.
Performs in continuous high temperatures with short-term capability up to 270°C.
Maintains friction surfaces in optimal condition, avoiding damage.
PTFE plating keeps out moisture and dust, reducing grease "throw" and improving seal performance.
Lower lubricant usage, fewer re-greasing intervals, and less equipment failure.
Safe to use in combination with existing grease systems.
Helps reduce labor, downtime, and lubricant volume — leading to cost savings.
