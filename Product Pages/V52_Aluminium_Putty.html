V52 ALUMINIUM PUTTY

OODA BOND V52 Aluminium Putty is an aluminium-filled, high-strength epoxy repair compound designed for non-rusting repairs to aluminium castings, parts, and equipment. It is ideal for use in HVAC systems, general machinery, and industrial maintenance tasks requiring a durable, metal-filled finish.

This two-part putty can be machined, drilled, or tapped using conventional tools once cured. It bonds to aluminium, steel, and many other metals, as well as concrete, and provides long-lasting, corrosion-resistant repairs.
